Short Analysis – Best Model

For both MNIST and FashionMNIST datasets, ResNet-18 consistently provided the best trade-off between accuracy and computational efficiency. While ResNet-50 is deeper, it did not yield significant accuracy improvements and incurred much higher training time and FLOPs, especially on CPU. Hence, ResNet-18 is the most suitable model for small-scale grayscale image classification tasks.
